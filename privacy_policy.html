Privacy Policy

SpendSense ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection and use of your information through the SpendSense mobile application.

1. Data Collection & Offline Privacy

SpendSense is a 100% offline application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal or financial data to external servers or third parties. All information you enter is stored locally and exclusively on your mobile device.

2. Types of Information Stored Locally

The following data is stored on your device's local storage to enable app functionality:

  • Transaction Details: Amount, category, date, and descriptions of expenses/income.
  • Budgeting Data: Financial goals and monthly budget limits.
  • System Preferences: App settings such as currency and theme preferences.

3. Use of Information

The data stored locally is used solely to provide the core services of the app, including:

  • Generating spending reports and visual charts.
  • Exporting data into PDF statements.
  • Displaying real-time updates via the Home Screen Widget.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Since the app operates without cloud synchronization, your data never leaves your device. We do NOT sell, share, or trade your information with any third-party services, advertisers, or analytics providers.

5. Security of Your Data

Because your data is stored locally, its security depends on the physical security of your device. We recommend using your device’s built-in security features (Passcode, Fingerprint, or Face ID) to prevent unauthorized access to your financial records.

6. User Control & Data Deletion

You have full ownership of your data. You can modify or delete your records at any time within the app. Please note that uninstalling the app or clearing app data will permanently delete all your financial records, as we do not maintain any backups on our end.

7. Children’s Privacy

SpendSense does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Our app is a general financial utility tool intended for all audiences.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any updates will be posted on this page and will be effective immediately upon posting.
