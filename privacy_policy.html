Privacy Policy

SpendSense ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information within the app.

1. Information We Collect

Our app is designed for offline usage. We may collect or store the following information locally on your device:

  • Transaction data (amount, category, date, and description).
  • Budget settings and financial goals.
  • Local app preferences and settings.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data collected is used solely to:

  • Provide expense tracking and budget management features.
  • Generate financial summaries and PDF statements.
  • Display spending insights via the home screen widget.

3. Data Storage & Privacy

Offline Storage: All financial data and expense records are stored locally on your mobile device. We do not upload your personal financial data to any external servers.

Security: Since the data stays on your device, its security depends on your device's overall security settings. We recommend using device locks (PIN/Fingerprint) to protect your information.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Since the app works offline, no data is transmitted to us or any third-party services.

5. User Rights & Data Control

You have full control over your data. You can edit or delete your expense records at any time within the app. Clearing the app storage or uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

SepndSense does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Our app is a general-purpose financial tool.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact our support team.
